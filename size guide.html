Size Guide

Using a shoe size chart: Compare your foot length to the chart: Use a chart that corresponds to your gender (men's or women's) and the desired size system (US, UK, Euro, etc.). Find your size: Locate the size on the chart that matches your measured foot length. Consider half sizes: If your foot length falls between two sizes, consider a half size up or down, depending on the shoe style and fit. Example: If your measured foot length is 24.5 cm, and you are looking for a US size, a US 7 or 7.5 would be a good fit for women's shoes. If you are looking for a UK size, a UK 6.5 would be a good fit. If you are looking for a Euro size, a Euro 39 would be a good fit. Additional Tips: Consider your foot shape: If you have a wider foot, you may need to go up a size. Try on shoes: If possible, try on shoes in the store to ensure a comfortable fit. Use a shoe shop: A shoe shop can provide accurate measurements and advise on sizing.
